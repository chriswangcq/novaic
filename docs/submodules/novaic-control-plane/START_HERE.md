# Start Here

If you are a team member, start here every day.

## 1) Find current round
- Open `CURRENT_ROUND.md`
- Follow the `round_path` link

## 2) Confirm your role
- Open `TEAM_MAP.md`
- Find your team section and owner

## 3) Claim and execute work
- Open your dispatch file in:
  - `rounds/<round-id>/10-dispatch/team-<name>.md`
- Execute mandatory tasks (implementation, not analysis only)

## 4) Submit your report
- Update your team report in:
  - `rounds/<round-id>/20-reports/team-<name>-report.md`
- Include required evidence:
  - exact command(s)
  - pass/fail summary
  - artifact/doc paths

## 5) Raise decision requests early
- If blocked or risky, fill `Decision Needed` in your report:
  - issue
  - options
  - recommendation
  - impact
  - owner
  - target_round

## Round cadence
- blocker sync happens during the round
- reports are submitted before round close

## Hard rule
- Understanding is not completion.
- No implementation evidence => cannot mark DONE.
