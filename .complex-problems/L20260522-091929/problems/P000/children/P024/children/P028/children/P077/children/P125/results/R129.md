# Production Queue Services Restarted In Postgres Mode

## Summary

Production Queue services were restarted from the clean cutover runtime in Postgres mode. Queue Service health reports `database_backend=postgres`, `/ready` returns 200, all expected Queue-related process roles are running, and no process holds `/opt/novaic/data/queue.db`.

## Done

- Started Queue Service from `/opt/novaic/services/novaic-agent-runtime-pg` on `127.0.0.1:19997` with Postgres backend and credential-file configuration.
- Started four task workers and two saga workers pointed at the restarted Queue Service.
- Started session outbox and saga outbox workers with Queue Postgres environment.
- Started Queue health worker and scheduler.
- Restarted business subscriber and gateway queue ingress.
- Saved redacted restart and verification reports.

## Verification

- Role counts: Queue Service 1, task workers 4, saga workers 2, session outbox 1, saga outbox 1, health 1, scheduler 1, business subscriber 1, gateway ingress 1.
- `/health`: healthy with `database_backend=postgres`.
- `/ready`: 200.
- Direct Queue DB processes have `NOVAIC_QUEUE_DB_BACKEND=postgres` and credential-file env set.
- `lsof /opt/novaic/data/queue.db`: no holder PIDs.
- Redaction scan over restart artifacts produced no credential matches or credential-file paths.
