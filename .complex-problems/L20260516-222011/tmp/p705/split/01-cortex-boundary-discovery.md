# Cortex boundary discovery and map

## Problem
Discover Cortex entrypoints, launch surfaces, semantic state/context modules, and dependencies on LogicalFS, Blob, Sandboxd, Queue, and Runtime. Produce an evidence-backed boundary map.

## Success Criteria
- Cortex entrypoint and launch evidence is listed.
- Context/scope/state modules are mapped to semantic ownership.
- LogicalFS/Blob/Sandboxd/Queue/Runtime usage is classified as dependency/orchestration rather than ownership.
- Generated or historical references are identified separately from active surfaces.
