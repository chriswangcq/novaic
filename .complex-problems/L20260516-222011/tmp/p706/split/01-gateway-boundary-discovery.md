# Gateway/app edge boundary discovery and map

## Problem
Discover Gateway and app edge entrypoints, launch wrappers, routing/API responsibilities, and dependencies on Business, Entangled, Blob, Device, Cortex, Queue, and Runtime. Produce an evidence-backed boundary map.

## Success Criteria
- Gateway/app entrypoints and launch references are listed.
- Gateway edge responsibilities are separated from Business, Entangled, Queue, Runtime, Device, Blob, and Cortex ownership.
- App/Tauri wrappers are classified as packaging/launch/client surfaces.
- Active/generated/historical references are separated and cleanup candidates are listed.
