# Devicectl Artifact-Producing Commands Contract Audit Result

## Summary

Audited artifact-producing devicectl paths for screenshot and file-pull. Existing implementation wraps device base64 responses by uploading decoded bytes to Blob Service and printing `tool-output.v1` artifact manifests. Focused fake-server tests confirm stdout omits raw `screenshot`/`data` and base64 payloads.

## Done

- Inspected `novaic-cortex/novaic_cortex/shell_capabilities.py` around `_wrap_hd_screenshot`, `_wrap_hd_file_pull`, `_wrap_hd_result`, and `_devicectl_hd`.
- Confirmed screenshot decodes `screenshot`, uploads bytes to Blob Service, and emits an image artifact manifest with `access.display.args.file_url`.
- Confirmed file-pull decodes `data` when `is_base64`, uploads bytes to Blob Service, and emits a binary artifact manifest.
- Confirmed diagnostics strip the raw screenshot field from screenshot output.
