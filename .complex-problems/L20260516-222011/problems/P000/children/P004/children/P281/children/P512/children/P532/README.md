# Classify Static Residue Hits

## Problem

Classify the raw P512 scan hits into live/expected, test-only, documentation/comment, or follow-up-worthy residue.

## Success Criteria

- Every raw hit is assigned a category or group with rationale.
- Production hits receive source-context review.
- Any risky production residue becomes a follow-up problem.
- No unclassified hit remains.
