# Health endpoint inventory and contract verification

## Problem Definition

Backend services may expose health endpoints. Need to inventory them across all services, verify their contracts, and check they return meaningful diagnostics.

## Proposed Solution

Grep across all submodules for health endpoint definitions (routes matching /health, /status, /ping patterns). Verify endpoint handlers exist and return structured responses. Cross-check with deploy/systemd health check configuration.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Health endpoints across all services located and listed.
- Endpoint contracts (paths, response shapes) verified against code.
- Stale or broken health paths identified (if any).

## Verification Plan

1. Grep for health/status/ping routes in all backend services.
2. Inspect handler code for response contracts.
3. Cross-check with systemd or deploy health check refs.

## Risks

- Some services may not have health endpoints (which is itself a finding).

## Assumptions

- Code-level inspection is sufficient without running services.
