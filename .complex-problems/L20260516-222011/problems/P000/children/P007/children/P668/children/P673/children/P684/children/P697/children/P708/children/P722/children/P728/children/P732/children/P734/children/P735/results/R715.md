# Device VmControl screenshot route classification result

## Summary

`novaic-device/device/vmcontrol_routes.py` is mounted by Device Service and exposes `/api/vmcontrol/vms/{vm_id}/screenshot`, but repository call-site search found no active Business/Gateway/Runtime/Cortex/App caller for that Device route. The actual VmControl side has `/api/vms/:id/screenshot` in the Tauri vmcontrol service. The Device wrapper route is therefore best classified as a mounted legacy/debug compatibility route and a remediation candidate, not as the active shell/devicectl screenshot path.

## Done

- Inspected `novaic-device/device/vmcontrol_routes.py`: the screenshot route returns `{"content": [{"type": "image", "data": result.get("data", ""), "mimeType": ...}]}` from `broker.vm_screenshot(vm_id)`.
- Verified the route is mounted in `novaic-device/main_device.py` via `from device.vmcontrol_routes import router as vmcontrol_router` and `app.include_router(vmcontrol_router)`.
- Searched for product/runtime references to `/api/vmcontrol`, `vmcontrol_routes`, `vm_screenshot`, and screenshot endpoint usage across Device, Business, Gateway, App, Runtime, Cortex, and docs.
- Found no active caller for Device `/api/vmcontrol/vms/{vm_id}/screenshot` outside the route itself and `pc_client.vm_screenshot`.
- Found the lower-level Tauri VmControl screenshot route at `novaic-app/src-tauri/vmcontrol/src/api/routes/mod.rs` and CloudBridge mapping in `novaic-app/src-tauri/vmcontrol/src/cloud_bridge.rs`; that is a separate VmControl-side route, not the Device wrapper route.
