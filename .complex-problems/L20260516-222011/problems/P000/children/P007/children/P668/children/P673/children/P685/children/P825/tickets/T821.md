## Problem Definition

Runbooks (cloud-production.md, local-backends.md, local-dev.md) describe operational procedures. Must match 8-service topology.

## Proposed Solution

Compare runbook service references against start.sh and P684 evidence.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Runbook service references match current topology.
- No stale instructions reference nonexistent services or ports.

## Verification Plan

- grep for ports and service names in runbooks.

## Risks

- none

## Assumptions

- start.sh and P684 are the source of truth.
