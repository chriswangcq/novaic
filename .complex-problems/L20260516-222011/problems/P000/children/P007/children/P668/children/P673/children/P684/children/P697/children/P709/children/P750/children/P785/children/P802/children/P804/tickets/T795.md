# App backend startup graph audit ticket

## Problem Definition
The app backend startup graph needs a read-only evidence map before remediation.

## Proposed Solution
Inspect startup scripts, packaged/generated variants, services metadata, port constants/usages, and committed backend resources. Record concrete inconsistencies and next cleanup targets without editing code.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Script variants are identified with paths and roles.
- Service/port graph is mapped from source evidence.
- Missing/stale resource expectations are listed.
- Remediation targets are concrete.

## Verification Plan
Use `find`, `rg`, `sed`, and `ls` only. Record paths and findings in the result.
