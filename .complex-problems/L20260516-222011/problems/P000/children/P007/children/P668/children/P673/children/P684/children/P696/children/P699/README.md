# Blob service boundary map

## Problem

Classify Blob as a foundational cheap file/object service. Verify active entrypoint, launch evidence, storage role, consumers, and boundaries against LogicalFS and Cortex.

## Success Criteria

- Blob service entrypoint and launch evidence are recorded with stable paths.
- Blob's role is summarized as byte/object storage, not live RO/RW workspace authority.
- Cortex and app/gateway consumers are identified as consumers, not ownership transfers.
- Misleading Blob boundary claims are patched or recorded.
